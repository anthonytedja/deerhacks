    what is deerhacks?

    deerhacks is...

    University of Toronto Mississauga's second annual hackathon where participants individually or in teams up to 4 are given 36 hours to demonstrate their creativity through their project! hackers of all levels are welcome to join us for a weekend of prizes, development, and innovation.

    participants will...

    be able to attend workshops, competitions, and fun activities to enjoy throughout the hackathon. participants will also have the opportunity to present their final project to a panel of experienced judges and industry veterans, as well as networking with sponsors and peers.

    the weekend of...

    January 1-3 2023 will be the event time for this year's hackathon. applications will open up on December 1. the hackathon will be fully in-person this year and the deadline to sign up will be on December 31 11:59pm.

    you should come...

    because you will learn something new, network and build connections, get rewarded for your hard work, and eat free food from our caterers! all you need to bring is your laptop (and possibly a sleeping bag to get comfy).
